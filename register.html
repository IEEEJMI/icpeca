Registration Details

  • At least one author from every accepted paper must register for the conference at the appropriate rate in their respective category in order for the research article to be included in the conference
  • A maximum of 6 pages per research article is allowed for submission. Extra pages are not permissible at this time
  • One registration by author will accommodate registration of 2 papers by the same registering author.
  • The Student Category registration covers a single paper registration only, even in the case that more than 1 paper by the same student author has been accepted
  • Separate registration will have to be made for each additional paper(s)
  • The registration fee will include the conference kit and lunch on all conference days
  • Authors will have to make arrangements for stay at Delhi
  • Authors who are unable to attend the conference in person may present their research work through online presentation (through Skype) in case the paper is selected for oral presentation. The author will still have to register under the appropriate category
  • Authors who require a separate VISA invitation letter, may request the same by e-mailing us at icpeca2019@jmi.ac.in
  • Authors are requested to complete the registration process before uploading the camera ready paper and e-copyright form through the conference management system found on the Submission page for this conference.
