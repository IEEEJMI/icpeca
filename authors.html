Instructions for Authors

All papers must be written in English.

Initial submission of paper (6 page max.) including figures and references are allowed in pdf format. Total file size of the paper must not exceed 2 MB. Accepted papers after the initial review will get the opportunity to submit full paper 6 pages max.

Please note:
  1. All papers submitted to ICPECA 2019 will go through a plagiarism check. papers with similarity scores of 20% or above are liable to be rejected without further review.
  2. All the submitted paper will go for double blind review.
  3. Authors don’t have to put names in paper submitted.
  4. Acceptance of the paper does not necessarily imply acceptance of the final paper.
The organizing committee of ICPECA2019 reserves the right to reject papers on the following grounds (one or more of):
  • Poor language
  • Poor technical content
  • Not addressing reviewer’s remarks
The detailed guidelines for the submission of final paper will be provided after acceptance notification is received.
